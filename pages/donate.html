You want to get better?

If you want to get better at weightlifting, the easiest thing to do is practice!! The more often you lift and the more comfortable you get in the gym, the better your experience will be and the better your body will feel! If you go often and you aren’t seeing any progress, try increasing the weight of your weights or adding on extra reps! You can do this!!!

Hot Tip!!!

Form is extremely important for keeping your body safe, if you have any pain when lifting, please stop and make sure you’re using the correct form and the correct weight for your strength level. If you can’t figure it out, take a break and see a trainer.
